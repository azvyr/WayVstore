Wavium Interactive Group

Wavium Interactive Group is an open, creator funded collective for independent game studios and solo developers. It exists to help developers build, ship, and grow real games through shared tools, structured competition, and community funded support.

Members use open source WayV technology, including the Wave Engine, while contributing to a professional guild that runs monthly game competitions, collaborative projects, mentorship, and a yearly flagship event with awards and funding. The group is designed to stay creator first, transparent, and independent from corporate control.

Wavium Interactive prioritizes small and mid sized teams, encourages experimentation, and reinvests membership fees directly back into developers through prize pools, grants, events, and shared resources. Larger studios may participate as supporters and sponsors, but governance and creative direction remain in the hands of the community.

The goal is simple: give creators structure, funding, and momentum without locking them into closed platforms or paywalled tools.
