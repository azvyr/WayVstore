Wave Engine

Wave Engine is a modern, open-source C++ 2D game engine developed and maintained by the Wavium Team at WayV. Designed for performance, portability, and extensibility, Wave Engine empowers developers to build advanced, feature-rich 2D games with ease.

Lightweight & Powerful

Wave Engine provides a robust foundation for both indie and professional game developers. Its architecture is designed for speed and flexibility, letting you build advanced, feature-rich 2D games with ease.

Open Source & Extensible

Wave Engine is fully open-source and maintained by the Wavium Team and the WayV community. Its extension market will allow you to add new features, tools, and even support for 3D games,built by us and the community.

Extension Marketplace

Wave Engine will feature a marketplace for extensions, enabling developers to add complete support for new features, platforms, and workflows. Whether you need advanced physics, 3D support, or custom tools, the extension market will make Wave Engine endlessly adaptable.
