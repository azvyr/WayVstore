Wave Engine

Wave Engine is a next-generation, high-performance game engine built in Rust for safety and speed. Featuring a data-oriented ECS architecture, blazing-fast GPU rendering, and seamless WASM scripting, it's designed for ambitious games and simulations.

  • Data-oriented ECS architecture
  • WASM scripting & extensibility
  • GPU-accelerated rendering
  • Open source, built for creators
