Wavium Interactive Group

Wavium Interactive Group is an open, creator-funded collective for indie studios and solo devs. We empower creators to build, ship, and grow real games through shared tools, monthly competitions, mentorship, and a vibrant community, all powered by open source WayV technology.

Not Yet Open to the Public

Wavium Interactive Group will open once Wave Engine is production-ready. Until then, we are focused on building the foundation for our creative community.

If you would like to sponsor Wavium Interactive Group, help build it, or join in the future, please email us at wayvsoftware@gmail.com.
